I'm a Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Washington, specializing in applying machine learning techniques and mechanical modeling in medical research. My current work focuses on machine learning in neuroimaging.

Specifically, I am now working on the construction of machine learning neuroimaging tools including:

  • (1) Developing innovative deep learning architectures to address computer vision challenges like lesion segmentation, presurgical and postsurgical registration, and synthesizing missing contrast in neuroimaging.
  • (2) Creating explainable deep learning frameworks for transparent surgical decision making in medical image classification and segmentation.
  • (3) Applying deep learning tools in clinical settings, particularly in predicting survival in glioblastoma patients using the explainable deep learning framework.
